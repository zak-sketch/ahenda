About Our Company

WHO WE ARE

AHENDA DRIPTECH LIMITED is an agricultural technology company headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya, helping both small-scale and large-scale farmers maximize productivity and profitability through innovative farming solutions.

WHAT WE DO

We provide farm structures, agronomical services, extension services, farm inputs, and market access under one trusted platform. Our comprehensive approach ensures farmers get all they need for successful farming operations.

Our Operational Principles

Clear guidelines for professional and efficient service delivery

1

Strict Time Management

Punctual service delivery and adherence to agreed timelines.

2

Professional Meetings

Meetings charged Kshs. 5000 per head per day for consultations.

3

Signed Agreement

Formal signed agreement required before commencement of work.

4

Official Invitation

Official client invitation letter required for site visits.

5

Transport & Logistics

Client caters for all transport and logistical arrangements.

6

Safety & Security

Client provides adequate safety and security measures.

7

Payment Structure

Payments: 40% deposit, 30% midway, 20% near completion, 10% final.

8

Binding Agreement

All principles are binding to both parties for smooth operations.
